OUR CLIENTS

Engr. Harvey A. Malolos is a licensed civil engineer and a consistent topnotcher. He placed first with a rating of 95.1% during the May 2014 Licensure Examination for Civil Engineers and graduated from Mapua Institute of Technology (MIT) as Cum Laude and Gold Medalist or top of his graduating class.

He started his career as structural design engineer in Maynilad Water Services, Inc., with specialization in structural design of water and wastewater structures, buildings, offices, and warehouses. He then worked in the Office of the Building Official in the City of Muntinlupa, holding two positions consecutively – former Head of the Civil/Structural Section and past Head of the Enforcement and Inspection Division. As the former, he has the authority to approve all civil/structural design plans of those applying for building permits.

He is also previously a faculty member in Mapua Institute of Technology, Far Eastern University – Institute of Technology, and Colegio de Muntinlupa, handling basic engineering sciences subjects, hydraulics, and structural analysis.

Engr. Norlan started his structural inspection career in Sy2 + Associates, Inc. His expertise includes visual inspection and identification of potential structural problems and deficiencies. He is also an expert in operating the Rebound Hammer, which is used to identify concrete uniformity, and the Rebar Scanner, which is utilized to properly locate and identify rebars in existing buildings.

Engr. Julius is a licensed civil engineer with affluent experience in structural design and inspection for four years already.

Mr. Medina has been into drafting for almost a decade. He was previously employed in Wellbuilt Steel Pte. Ltd. in Singapore as structural detailer. He’s knowledgeable in both structural concrete and steel detailing.

Ar. Emman has been a licensed architect for 14 years. He has professional experience in architectural design, general contracting, and project management.

Engr. Jojo has been in practice for 10 years, specializing in both design and installation of electro-mechanical components.

Engr. Ava has been in practice for 8 years already. She previously worked in a prestigious MEPFS design firm in the Philippines, and has experience in the design of sanitary, plumbing and fire protection of mid-rise to high-rise buildings.
